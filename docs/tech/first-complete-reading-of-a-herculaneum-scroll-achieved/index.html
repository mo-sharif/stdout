02 · The Method

High-tech imaging and AI to reveal hidden text

The scroll was scanned with high-resolution X-rays, reconstructed digitally, and machine learning brought out faint ink traces.

It is the first Herculaneum papyrus to be digitally unrolled and read in full.S1

03 · The Content

A Stoic philosophical treatise uncovered

The recovered text is a philosophical treatise on ethics, likely a Stoic work attributed to Aristocreon, dating to the 2nd century BC.

It turns on human nature, impulse, and the moral progress of human beings.S1
04 · The Impact

Open data and scalable method for future scrolls

All tomographic data, reconstructions, transcriptions, and code are openly available under a Creative Commons licence.

Anyone can check the work, build on it, and apply it to the scrolls that remain.S1
